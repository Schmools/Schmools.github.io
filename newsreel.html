Newsreel

Newsreel was a project I worked on for HackTrin V, a hackathon at the Trinity school in NYC with my friend Pratham. The project was a news website that used crowdsourced data from the Twitter API and other sources to rank news articles by sentiment towards them and biases that they may have. I mostly worked on the front end and graphic design of the website, designing a whole bunch of related logos and icons in illustrator and putting them together to make a visually appealing and clean website. Newsreel won first place, getting the Judges Prize; you can find out more about Newsreel here: https://devpost.com/software/newsreel-ahm5zc!
