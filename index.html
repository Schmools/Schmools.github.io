Hi, I'm Samuel Schuur, a student at the University of Chicago studying maths. Previously I've worked at the Center for Bits and Atoms at MIT, building tools for flexible computing and manufacturing workflows, as well as at Elmworks in Berkeley, CA developing wire plotting tools for building high frequency electromagnetic devices. I have a mild obsession with pretty much everything with any relation to engineering, robotics, and CNCs. I fold origami. I also play the violin. This website is frequently (and currently) out of date in terms of projects I've worked on but at least gives at taste of my interests - I promise to update it soon.
